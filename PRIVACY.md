## 2. Information We Collect

### A. Personal Identification Data
* **Email Address & Password:** When you create an account to back up and sync your logs, we store your email address. Password validation is managed directly by Google Firebase Authentication. Passwords are encrypted on Google's servers and are never accessible to us.
* **Profile Username:** An optional display name/nickname you choose to personalize greetings and stats.

### B. Special Category Personal Data (GDPR Article 9)
* **Sadhana & Journal Logs:** You may log spiritual and religious practices (e.g. Mantras, Stotras, Chalisas), including dates performed, daily counts, and optional personal reflections (`notes`). 
* **Special Category Data:** Because spiritual or religious logging may reveal your religious or philosophical beliefs, this information is classified as **Special Category Data** under GDPR Article 9. We process this data *only* with your explicit consent (see Section 3).

### C. Client-Side Browser Storage (Local Storage)
* **localStorage Cache:** The app uses local storage on your web browser to store settings and logs. 
* **Guest Mode:** If you do not sign in, all data is stored 100% locally on your browser and is never sent to the cloud.
* **Authenticated Mode:** If you sign in, local storage is used to cache your cloud database. To protect your privacy on shared devices, logging out of your account will **completely clear** the local storage cache from the device.

---

## 3. Lawful Basis for Processing (GDPR Article 6 & 9)

We process your data under the following lawful bases:
* **Explicit Consent (Article 6(1)(a) & Article 9(2)(a)):** By voluntarily configuring practices, logging counts, and writing reflections, you give us explicit consent to process your spiritual journal entries (Special Category Data). You may withdraw this consent at any time by deleting your entries, your practices, or your account.
* **Performance of Contract (Article 6(1)(b)):** For signed-in users, syncing and backing up your journal data to the cloud is necessary to perform the service you requested.
* **Legitimate Interests (Article 6(1)(f)):** Processing technical server logs for network security, DDoS mitigation, and system stability.

---

## 4. Sub-Processors and Data Infrastructure

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal journal data. However, we utilize the following third-party sub-processors to host and run our infrastructure:

1. **Google LLC (Firebase):** Hosts our Authentication and Cloud Firestore database. Google processes your account email and database entries under secure compliance frameworks.
2. **Vercel Inc.:** Hosts the frontend static web assets and serves the user interface.

### Infrastructure Log Disclosure (IP Addresses)
While the Sadhana Journal application code does not track, collect, or store your IP address or geographic location, our infrastructure sub-processors (Vercel and Google Firebase) automatically log standard network metadata (such as IP addresses, request headers, user-agent strings, and request timestamps) at the server level. This is done to ensure network safety, mitigate DDoS attacks, enforce rate-limiting, and debug server health.

---

## 5. International Data Transfers

Google Cloud/Firebase and Vercel utilize global data centers, which may result in your data being stored or processed outside the European Economic Area (EEA), including in the United States. 

To safeguard these transfers, our sub-processors comply with **Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)** approved by the European Commission, ensuring your data receives an equivalent level of protection as it would within the EU.

---

## 6. Data Retention Period

* **Guest Users:** Data remains on your device until you clear your browser cache or delete the site data.
* **Authenticated Users:** Your account details and logs are retained for as long as you maintain your account. 
* **Deletion:** If you delete a log entry, a vow, or a custom practice, it is deleted from both your local browser cache and the cloud database immediately. If you request account deletion, all personal data is deleted from Firebase Authentication and Firestore within 30 days.

---

## 7. Your Privacy Rights

Under the GDPR, you have the following rights:
* **Right of Access & Portability:** You can view all your logged entries directly on your dashboard.
* **Right to Rectification:** You can edit your logs, adjust counts, and modify display names in the settings at any time.
* **Right to Erasure:** You have the right to request deletion of your account and all associated cloud data.
* **Right to Lodge a Complaint:** If you believe we have violated data privacy regulations, you have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority (a Data Protection Authority) in your country of residence.

This application is not intended for use by minors. You must be at least 16 years of age (or the minimum legal age of consent in your country of residence) to register an account and sync data to the cloud. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
